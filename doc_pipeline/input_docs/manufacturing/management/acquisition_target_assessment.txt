Target: PrecisionCore Bearings LLC (private, Ohio)

1. STRATEGIC RATIONALE

   PrecisionCore (PC) is a regional precision-bearing manufacturer with
   ~$28M revenue and specialty capabilities in P4-class spindle bearings
   that we currently source single-source from SKF. A successful close
   would:

     a) Eliminate the single-source exposure on CNC spindle parts that
        accounts for $1.1M of annual tariff risk.
     b) Bring spindle bearing manufacturing in-house, reducing landed
        cost by an estimated 22-28% on the affected SKUs.
     c) Add 18,000 sq ft of grinding and lapping capacity in Ohio that
        could absorb future precision-machining demand from our Line 5
        expansion (FY27).

2. FINANCIAL PROFILE OF TARGET

   FY25 Revenue ...................... $27.8M
   FY25 EBITDA ....................... $4.1M  (14.8% margin)
   FY25 Free Cash Flow ............... $2.6M
   Net Debt .......................... $0.4M
   Working capital % of revenue ...... 18%

   Pre-LOI valuation range:
     - 6.5x EBITDA (low / strategic floor) .......... $26.7M
     - 8.0x EBITDA (mid / negotiated base case) ..... $32.8M
     - 9.5x EBITDA (high / competing-bid scenario) .. $39.0M

   Recommended walk-away: $36M enterprise value (8.8x EBITDA).

     COGS reduction (in-house spindle bearings) ..... $1.6M

4. KEY DILIGENCE FINDINGS

   - Customer concentration is acceptable: top 5 customers are 41% of
     revenue, no single customer >12%.
   - Two key engineers (lead grinding metallurgist, P4-class process
     engineer) are critical-person risks; retention packages required
     pre-signing.
   - Environmental: one historical solvent release on the south pad
     (closed by EPA in 2018) — Phase II ESA recommends no further
     remediation. Reserve $0.2M for residual indemnity.
   - IT: legacy MES needs replatforming (~$0.6M, 6 months) post-close.

Work Order: wo:asrs:989002
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
both the b757 flight crew and their dispatcher misinterpreted the minimum amount of fuel required in the affected tank when dispatched with the aft boost pump inoperative.

## Resolution
The language was clearly ambiguous and the reporter's suggestions to mirror the language utilized for the bigger, but operationally similar, twin jet are entirely appropriate.

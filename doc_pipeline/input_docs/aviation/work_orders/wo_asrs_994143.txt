Work Order: wo:asrs:994143
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
an md11 flight crew was dispatched with an mel requiring manual fuel pump operation. passing fl180 the center engine lost power and eventually flamed out. the first officer discovered that the fuel pumps are not on.they were turned on, along with ignition. the engine started up and the flight continued to destination.

## Resolution
Apparently all of the pumps were off and the center engine flamed out first due to its' location above the fuel tanks. Not good. There should have been some low pressure lights on the over head panel to indicate the pumps were not on. This MEL should have induced the crew to place a memory jogger in plain view to prevent forgetting the fuel pumps.

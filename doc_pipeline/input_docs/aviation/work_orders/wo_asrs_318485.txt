HE BELIEVES THAT ACFT OWNERS ARE ONLY TAUGHT TO FLY AND NOT ABOUT THE RESPONSIBILITY OF MAINTAINING THE ACFT. HE HOPES THAT THERE WILL BE ARTICLES WRITTEN ON THIS SUBJECT.

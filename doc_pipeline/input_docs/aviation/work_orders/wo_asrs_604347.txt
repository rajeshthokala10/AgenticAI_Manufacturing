Work Order: wo:asrs:604347
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a b737-300 was dispatched in non compliance with #1 ignition deferred per the mel. special procs for swapping wiring were not accomplished correctly.

## Resolution
WHILE PERFORMING MEL PROCS ON ACFT #1 ENG, MEL 74-1 IGNITION, I MISIDENTED THE WIRES THAT WERE TO BE REQUIRED BY MEL 74-1, SP-74. APPARENTLY, THIS CAUSED THE AUTO SPD BRAKE NOT TO AUTO ARM (GREEN LIGHT). NO OTHER INDICATIONS WERE NOTED. THE PROB WAS CORRECTED AT THE NEXT STOP.

Work Order: wo:asrs:1141914
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
md11 flight crew is informed by the tower during landing roll that fluid was observed streaming from the left engine during approach and continues. the left engine is shut down with no change in the fluid flow. all engines are shut down and the aircraft is towed to the gate.

## Resolution
Apparently a fuel leak on the left engine. If shutting down the left engine had no effect on the leak then the fire button should have been pushed cutting off fuel at the tank. The missing fuel should have been enough to trigger a fuel imbalance alert.

Work Order: wo:asrs:417187
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
an emb120 aborted tkof twice due to #1 engine electronic engine ctl failure.

## Resolution
ABORTED TKOF TWICE WHEN ELECTRONIC ENG CTL FAILED ONLY AFTER CONDITION LEVERS MAX AND PWR BEING APPLIED COMMENCING TKOF ROLL. FIRST ABORT ROUTINE WITH NORMAL ELECTRONIC ENG CTL RESET AND TAXI BACK. SECOND ABORT ABNORMAL WITH #1 ENG/PROP NOT PROPERLY RESPONDING TO PWR REDUCTION AND REVERSE/BETA POS, WITH ENG CONTINUING TO PRODUCE EXCESS THRUST, SUCH THAT FEATHERING #1 PROP BEST FACILITATED EXPEDITING CLR OF RWY FOR LNDG TFC. AFTER CLRING RWY AND SELECTING ELECTRONIC ENG CTL MANUAL, ABNORMAL HIGH GND IDLE PWR CONDITION CEASED. ELECTRONIC ENG CTL 'S WERE DEFERRED PER COMPANY MAINT CTL AND FLT RESUMED LATE BUT ROUTINE WITH ELECTRONIC ENG CTL SELECTED MANUAL.

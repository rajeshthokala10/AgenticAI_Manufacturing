Work Order: wo:asrs:622227
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
b757 flt crew mismanage their fuel sys, landing with most of their unused fuel in the ctr tank at zzz, us.

## Resolution
THE CTR TANK FUEL QUANTITY INDICATOR DISPLAYED AN LCD ABERRATION (HOT SPOT) WITH RESPECT TO THE FARTHEST L DIGIT WHICH MADE IT POSSIBLE TO MISREAD THE CTR TANK QUANTITY BY 10000 LBS (IE, 11.0 COULD BE INTERPED AS 1.0). DURING CLB, THE MONITORING PLT SELECTED CTR PUMPS ON TO BURN THE CTR TANK FUEL LEVEL TO 1.0 AS PER THE CURRENT 'FUEL USAGE RESTRS A.D.' AT APPROX TOP OF CLB, THE MONITORING PLT STATED 'CTR PUMPS OFF.' AS THE PF, I OBSERVED WHAT APPEARED TO ME TO BE 1.0 ON THE CTR TANK GAUGE. THE FLT PROCEEDED NORMALLY UNTIL APPROX TOP OF DSCNT WHEN FMC ANNUNCIATED 'LOW FUEL.' I IMMEDIATELY SELECTED CTR PUMPS ON AND VERIFIED THAT THE TOTAL QUANTITY WAS APPROX 16.4. CLOSER EXAMINATION REVEALED THAT THE AMOUNT IN THE CTR TANK WAS IN FACT 11.0. THE FLT PROCEEDED NORMALLY AND LANDED WITHOUT FURTHER ABNORMALITY. FUEL WAS MONITORED UNTIL PARKING WITH LEVELS OF APPROX 2.5 L MAIN, 2.9 R MAIN AND 8.3 CTR.

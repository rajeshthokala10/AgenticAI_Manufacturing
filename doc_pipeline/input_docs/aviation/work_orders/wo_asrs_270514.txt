Work Order: wo:asrs:270514
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
plt of a sma sel return to land with atc emer handling after the engine lost pwr 2 times during initial climb after tkof.

## Resolution
THE ENG WAS DIFFICULT TO START AND TOOK SOME TIME TO WARM UP. THE ENG PASSED THE MAGNETO CHK DURING RUN UP AND ALL ENG INSTS READ NORMAL. SHORTLY AFTER TKOF (200 FT AGL) THE ENG HICCUPPED. PWR RETURNED AND THEN DROPPED AGAIN AT ABOUT (500 FT AGL), THEN PWR RETURNED AGAIN. WE RPTED A ROUGH RUNNING ENG TO ATC AND WERE IMMEDIATELY CLRED FOR ANY RWY BY THE TWR AND THE TWR CLOSED THE ARPT UNTIL WE WERE ON THE GND. NO FURTHER LOSS OF PWR WAS EXPERIENCED AFTER THE DECISION WAS MADE TO RETURN TO THE ARPT.

Work Order: wo:asrs:1049932
Subsystem: both
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
pa44 instructor pilot reports shutting down the right engine at 5,500 feet for training then being unable to restart it after three tries. an emergency is declared but during descent a fourth attempt to restart is successful and the emergency is canceled.

## Resolution
The air temperature was likely very cold during this event making the restart more difficult, but persistence pays at least in this case.

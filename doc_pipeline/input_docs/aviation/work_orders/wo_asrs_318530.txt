## Complaint
piper navajo has engine fire in flt.

## Resolution
PA31-350 PIPER NAVAJO. L ENG FIRE, I THINK WAS CAUSED BY OIL LINE NOT PROPERLY TIGHTENED. SHUTTING OFF FUEL DID NOT EXTINGUISH FLAME. CALLBACK CONVERSATION WITH RPTR REVEALED THE FOLLOWING INFO: RPTR STATES HE WAS ABOUT 4 MI FROM THE ARPT AND GOT THE ACFT DOWN AS FAST AS POSSIBLE. THE INSURANCE COMPANY TOTALED THE ACFT SINCE THE ENG WAS RUINED AND THEY WERE CONCERNED THAT FIRE HAD WEAKENED THE WING SPAR. THE FIRE WAS ON THE R SIDE OF THE L ENG AND AWAY FROM THE FUEL TANKS. RPTR FEELS STRONGLY THAT THE OIL LINE WAS LOOSE, ONLY FINGER TIGHT, AS IT WAS LYING ON THE BOTTOM OF THE COWLING. NO INJURIES INVOLVED.

Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
aircraft equip prob - the student rented an aircraft for an inst lesson and the instructor questioned the acft's airworthiness, but the student assured him based on the fbo employee's statements. the instructor was informed that the aircraft was gnded.

## Resolution
THE PERSON RENTING THE ACFT PRESENTED THE ACFT FOR HIS INST LESSON WHICH HE HAD SCHEDULED WITH ME. HE SAID THAT THE CYLINDER HEAD TEMP GAUGE WAS INOP AND THE DME, IS INTERMITTENT. I SAID ARE YOU SURE THIS ACFT ISN'T GNDED. HE SAID THAT THE FAA HAD SAID IT WAS OK TO FLY LIKE THIS. DUE TO THE ADDITIONAL INSTALLATION FOR CHT (GEM) THE ENG MONITOR, I FLEW THE LESSON. ON APR/TUE/95 I WAS INFORMED THAT THE ACFT WAS GNDED ON THE DAY OF MY FLT. ALTHOUGH I DIDN'T SEE A GNDING SIGN ON IT. BECAUSE THE PERSON FURNISHING THE ACFT WAS PIC, AND RENTING THE ACFT. I DIDN'T SEE ANY DISCREPANCIES FROM A DISCREPANCY SHEET. I FEEL THE FBO FAILED TO ENSURE THAT THEY DIDN'T RENT AN ACFT WHICH HAD BEEN SUPPOSEDLY GNDED. I DIDN'T SEE ANY EVIDENCE OF THIS GNDING.

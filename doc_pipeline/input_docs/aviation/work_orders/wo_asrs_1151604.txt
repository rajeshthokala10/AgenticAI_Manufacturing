Work Order: wo:asrs:1151604
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
experimental aircraft pilot experiences a loss of engine power and elects to land on a highway. no damage to the aircraft or property is reported. carburetor ice is the suspected cause of the power loss.

## Resolution
Carburetor should be one of the first considerations with a gradual power loss. Carburetor heat will correct this problem.

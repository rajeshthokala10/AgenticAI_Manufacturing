Work Order: wo:asrs:426345
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
pvt plt of an sel sma made an emer landing on an interstate hwy after the engine quit due to fuel exhaustion.

## Resolution
AFTER 3.3 HRS OF FLT FROM TUS TO PSP I EXPERIENCED FUEL EXHAUSTION AND LANDED ON INTERSTATE 10 FREEWAY WITHOUT INCIDENT AND WITHOUT DAMAGE. THE OWNER'S MANUAL INDICATED THAT I SHOULD HAVE HAD 4.2 HRS OF ENDURANCE AT MY INDICATED RPM OF 2600. THE HWY PATROL OBTAINED FAA PERMISSION FOR ME TO TAKE OFF FROM THE FREEWAY AFTER OBTAINING FUEL. THE HWY PATROL ESTABLISHED A TFC BREAK AND WE PROCEEDED ON WITHOUT INCIDENT AFTER TKOF.

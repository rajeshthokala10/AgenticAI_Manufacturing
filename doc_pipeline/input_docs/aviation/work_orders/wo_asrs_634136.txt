Work Order: wo:asrs:634136
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a super decathlon developed a rough running engine 25 mins into a flt. a successful precautionary landing followed. fouled spark plugs were discovered.

## Resolution
THE ENG, ON MY SUPER DECATHLON, BEGAN TO RUN ROUGH. I IMMEDIATELY TURNED TOWARD ZZZ1 ARPT AND AS A PRECAUTIONARY MEASURE NOTIFIED, CALLED ZZZ1 ARPT. I INFORMED THEM THAT I HAD A ROUGH RUNNING ENG AND WOULD LIKE TO LAND ON RWY 29R AS SOON AS POSSIBLE. I DID NOT FEEL THAT THERE WAS AN EMER. THEY GAVE ME CLRNC TO LAND AND I LANDED WITHOUT INCIDENT. THIS WAS THE SECOND FLT OF THE ACFT AFTER THE ANNUAL BY MAINT. BEFORE DEPARTING FROM ZZZ1, I DID A MAGNETO CHK WHICH WAS NORMAL. ON DEP FROM RWY 29R THE ENG WAS RUNNING NORMALLY. IT WAS NOT UNTIL ABOUT 25 MINS INTO THE FLT THAT THE ENG BEGAN TO RUN ROUGH. ON LNDG I TAXIED TO THE MAINT FOR ENG ANALYSIS. THEY FOUND THAT TWO OF THE BOTTOM SPARK PLUGS WERE FOULED. THEY CLEANED THE SPARK PLUGS AND DID A MAGNETO CHK WHICH WAS NORMAL. I HAVE SINCE FLOWN THE ACFT AND FOUND NO OTHER PROBS.

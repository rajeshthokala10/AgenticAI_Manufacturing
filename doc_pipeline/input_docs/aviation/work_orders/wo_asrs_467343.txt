Work Order: wo:asrs:467343
Subsystem: both
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
pvt plt of a piper pa28-161 caught engine on fire during attempting to start the engine in an open hangar early in the morning. rptr used cold wx starting procs and subsequently extinguished the fire.

## Resolution
AT ABOUT XA00 AM ON THE MORNING OF MAR/SAT/00, I WAS PREPARING TO TAKE A FLT WITH 2 FRIENDS TO KALAMAZOO. SMA WAS STORED IN THE ROUND HANGAR, LOCATED BEHIND THE NE T-HANGARS AT ANN ARBOR ARPT. WHILE MY FRIENDS WAITED FOR ME AT THE FBO AT THE ANN ARBOR ARPT, I WENT TO THE HANGAR TO START THE ACFT. I COMPLETED THE PREFLT INSPECTION, INCLUDING CHKING THE OIL, AND UNPLUGGED THE ENG BLOCK HEATER. NEXT, I BEGAN THE COLD START PROC. I PRIMED THE ENG ONCE, THEN USED THE STARTER FOR SEVERAL SECONDS. THE ENG TURNED OVER, BUT DID NOT ENGAGE. I WAITED FOR A BRIEF PERIOD, AND CRANKED THE STARTER AGAIN FOR SEVERAL SECONDS. ONCE AGAIN, THE ENG TURNED OVER BUT DID NOT ENGAGE. AFTER THE SECOND CRANKING ATTEMPT I SHUT THE FUEL PUMP AND MASTER SWITCH OFF, AS WELL AS CUT THE MIXTURE AND CLOSED THE THROTTLE. I THEN WAITED FOR A FEW MINS. I ATTEMPTED TO START THE ENG AGAIN USING THE COLD START CHKLIST. I PRIMED THE ENG FOR THE SECOND TIME, AND USED THE STARTER AGAIN. THIS TIME, THE ENG FIRED IRREGULARLY FOR A FEW SECONDS, THEN STOPPED. WITHOUT PRIMING AGAIN, I CRANKED THE STARTER, AND THE ENG FIRED BRIEFLY, SOMEWHAT LONGER THAN THE FIRST TIME, AND STOPPED YET AGAIN. I SHUT DOWN THE FUEL PUMP AND TURNED THE MASTER SWITCH OFF, AND WAITED FOR SEVERAL MINS. FOR THE THIRD AND LAST TIME, I WENT THROUGH THE COLD ENG STARTING PROC, INCLUDING PRIMING THE ENG. THIS TIME THE ENG NEVER ENGAGED. AFTER THIS I GAVE UP ATTEMPTING TO START THE PLANE. I TURNED OFF THE MASTER SWITCH AND FUEL PUMP, CLOSED THE MIXTURE, AND GOT OUT OF THE PLANE TO TELL MY FRIENDS THAT WE WOULD HAVE TO WAIT FOR THE ENG TO WARM UP BEFORE WE COULD START THE PLANE. AS SOON AS I GOT OUT OF THE PLANE I SAW A SMALL AMOUNT OF THIN WHITE SMOKE COMING OUT OF THE ENG COWLING BY THE PROP AND THE CARB AIR INTAKE. I QUICKLY GOT BACK INTO THE PLANE AND TRIED TO CRANK THE ENG WITH FULL THROTTLE AND THE MIXTURE LEANED COMPLETELY. I COULD SEE MORE SMOKE COMING FROM THE COWLING, AND DECIDED THAT IT WAS TOO LATE TO SUCK THE FIRE BACK INTO THE ENG, AND I DID NOT FEEL SAFE SITTING IN A PLANE WITH AN ENG FIRE. I TURNED OFF THE MASTER AND QUICKLY GOT OUT OF THE PLANE. THERE WAS NO FIRE EXTINGUISHER IN THE ROUND HANGAR WITH THE PLANE. I RAN ABOUT 100 YARDS BACK TO FBO TO GET A FIRE EXTINGUISHER. I PULLED THE EXTINGUISHER FROM THE WALL AND RAN BACK TO THE PLANE, DURING WHICH TIME I APPARENTLY LOST ONE SHOE. WHEN I REACHED THE ACFT, IT HAD FLAMES COMING OUT OF THE AIR INTAKE COWLING. HOWEVER, THE FIRE EXTINGUISHER I HAD GOTTEN FROM FBO DID NOT WORK. IT PUFFED FOR A BRIEF SECOND AND DIED. I THEN RAN TO THE CITY MAINT BUILDING, WHICH WAS ACTUALLY CLOSER, ALTHOUGH I DID NOT THINK IT LIKELY THAT IT WOULD BE OPEN AT THAT TIME ON SATURDAY MORNING. HOWEVER, THE FRONT DOOR WAS OPEN, AND I GOT THEIR EXTINGUISHER. I RAN BACK TO THE ROUND HANGAR, AND MANAGED TO PUT OUT THE FIRE.

Work Order: wo:asrs:373676
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
an atr42 at 500 ft agl on final approach experienced a pwr loss in #2 engine caused by ignition sys failure.

## Resolution
LOST PWR ON #2 ENG ON SHORT FINAL. COMPLETED IMMEDIATE ACTION ITEMS, NOTIFIED TWR, UNEVENTFUL SINGLE ENG LNDG. CALLBACK CONVERSATION WITH RPTR REVEALED THE FOLLOWING INFO: RPTR STATED THE ACFT WAS AN ATR42 POWERED WITH P&W 121A ENGS. RPTR ALSO SAID THAT MAINT FOUND THE #2 ENG IGNITION SYS AT FAULT AND REPLACED AN EXCITER BOX AND CLEANED THE IGNITER PLUGS. RPTR ALSO STATED THE ACFT WAS RUN AFTER MAINT AND OPERATED OK.

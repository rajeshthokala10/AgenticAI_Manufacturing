Work Order: wo:asrs:918053
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a crj200's cas alerted fuel imbalance and the crew began the qrh fuel leak procedure, declared an emergency, descended and diverted to a nearby airport.

## Resolution
FUEL IMBALANCE CAS message at FL320. Referred QRH and as per QRH, with auto transferflow still operative, we went to 7-10 Fuel Leak Procedure. Declared emergency with Center in order to get an expedited decent. During decent to FL 180 noted a fuel imbalance at 1,000 LBS between tanks (right tank low). Below 10,000 auto transferflow started correcting imbalance. Landed nearest suitable airport. Contacted Maintenance and coordinated with Operations to continue the flight to our planned destination with another aircraft already available. Captain coordinated for landing with Operations, Dispatch and Flight Attendant. I continued flying and working radios.

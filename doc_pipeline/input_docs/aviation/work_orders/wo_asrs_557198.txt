Work Order: wo:asrs:557198
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
b767-300 crew had the ctr fuel tank not feed fuel properly.

## Resolution
AFTER LEVELOFF, WE NOTICED L CTR FUEL TANK PUMP WAS NOT WORKING PROPERLY. THIS WAS CAUSING A FUEL IMBALANCE (IN WING TANKS). WE COMMUNICATED WITH DISPATCH OUR DESIRE TO DIVERT TO EGLL, WHICH WE DID. WE DUMPED FUEL, BUT STILL LANDED OVER MAX ALLOWABLE LNDG WT (344000 VERSUS 320000 LBS). BECAUSE OF THAT, I REQUESTED EMER EQUIP TO STAND BY. FOLLOWED SOP. UNEVENTFUL LNDG.

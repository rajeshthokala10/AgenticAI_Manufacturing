## Complaint
sma has engine failure after tkof. off arpt landing

## Resolution
ENG STOPPED WHILE AT 1500 FT AGL SHORTLY AFTER TKOF FROM APPLE VALLEY ARPT. I BROADCAST ON 122.8 (UNICOM) THAT I HAD A MAYDAY SIT WITH A STOPPED ENG AND WOULD TRY TO RETURN TO THE ARPT. I REALIZED I WOULD NOT MAKE IT SAFELY AND REBROADCAST THAT I WAS LNDG IN THE DRY LAKE BED N OF APPLE VALLEY CITY. SUCCESSFUL LNDG ON LAKE BED. CONNECTING ROD IN ENG FAILED WHICH CAUSED STOPPAGE.

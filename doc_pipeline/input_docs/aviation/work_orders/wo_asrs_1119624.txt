Work Order: wo:asrs:1119624
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a pilot in a king air was distracted during a maintenance flight with a mechanic, and neglected to activate the auto ignition switches. when he noticed, he reached for the switches but unintentionally activated the starter generators, removing generator power from the electrical system. after a successful return to the departure airport, he discovered that his radio was unable to contact ground control, likely due to the exhaustion of the battery power.

## Resolution
Good thinking to get back on the ground ASAP. This pilot will likely not be distracted again by non-essential activities.

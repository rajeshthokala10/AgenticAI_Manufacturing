Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a b737-300 was dispatched in non compliance with 2 fuel boost pumps inop -- 1 pump in each main tank -- in conflict with the mel requirement.

## Resolution
2 AFT FUEL PUMPS DEFERRED, FRESH MAINT RELEASE, ALL DEFERRED ITEMS WITH STICKERS AND REF NUMBERS. I THOUGHT THAT 1 FUEL PUMP PER MAIN TANK MUST BE OK WITH MAINT TO DEFER. NO LIMITATION IN FLT MANUAL FOR MAIN TANK PUMPS. INFORMED BY DISPATCH VIA COMMERCIAL RADIO TOWARD END OF FLT TO CALL. FLT WAS ILLEGAL BECAUSE APPARENTLY ONLY 1 OF 4 MAY BE DEFERRED. MY TRUST IN WHAT APPEARED TO BE A CLEAN LOGBOOK WAS MISPLACED. ADDITIONAL POSTFLT DISCUSSIONS WITH MY FO AFTER FINDING OUT OUR ILLEGAL DISPATCH WITH THIS FLT LEFT ME WITH NO OTHER IDEAS AS TO HOW TO HAVE PREVENTED THIS.

Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a320 crew had a suspected fuel leak in zab class a.

## Resolution
LEVELED OFF OUT OF LAX AND RECEIVED AN AUTO FUEL FEED FAULT ON ECAM, SWITCHED TO MANUAL AND NOTICED THAT LEFT TANK HAD BURNED OUT EXCESSIVELY DURING CLIMB, SWITCH TO CROSS FEED FOR BALANCING AND TURNED OFF LEFT BOOST PUMPS, FUEL CONTINUED TO DECREASE FROM LEFT TANK, SUSPECTED A FUEL LEAK, DECLARED EMER, PREPARED CABIN FOR EVAC, DIVERTED TO PHX UNEVENTFULLY.

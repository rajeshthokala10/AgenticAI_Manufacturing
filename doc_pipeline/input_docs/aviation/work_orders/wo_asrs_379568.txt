Work Order: wo:asrs:379568
Subsystem: both
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
the plts on an smt rpt that the pic was making a gar when the l engine failed. the pic then changed his mind and made a successful straight-in landing another aircraft with com failure, was landing on the parallel runway

## Resolution
CLRED FOR VISUAL APCH, CTLR INSTRUCTED PIC TO SLOW TO MINIMUM AIRSPD, SLOWER TFC WAS TURNING BASE TO FINAL IN FRONT OF US ON RWY 9L. ATC INSTRUCTED PIC TO MAINTAIN ALT ON SHORT FINAL AND TO AWAIT LNDG CLRNC. PIC DECIDED TO EXECUTE GAR PROCS. UPON INITIATING GAR, THE L ENG FAILED. PIC FEATHERED INOP ENG AND LANDED SAFELY WITHOUT EVENT. CONTRIBUTING TO SIT WAS ANOTHER ACFT WITH A COM FAILURE LNDG ON PARALLEL RWY. CAUTION SHOULD BE EXERCISED WHEN ATC CLRS FOR VISUAL APCH WITH OTHER ACFT IN VICINITY. VECTORING OR HOLDING SHOULD BE UTILIZED RATHER THAN MULTIPLE ACFT APCHING SAME RWY AT SAME TIME. SUPPLEMENTAL INFO FROM ACN 379569: UPON BEING CLRED FOR THE VISUAL APCH, CTLR TOLD PIC TO SLOW ACFT. SLOWED TFC WAS LNDG RWY 9L FROM BASE TO FINAL. I WAS TOLD TO MAINTAIN ALT APPROX 500 FT AGL. I MADE DECISION TO GAR DUE TO SLOWER ACFT. AFTER STARTING THE GAR MY L ENG FAILED AND I FEATHERED THE L ENG AND CONTINUED TO AN UNEVENTFUL LNDG.

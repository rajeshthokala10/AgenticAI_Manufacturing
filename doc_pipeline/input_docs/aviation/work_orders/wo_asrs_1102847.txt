Work Order: wo:asrs:1102847
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
captain reports a 5,000 lbs wing lateral fuel imbalance on their md-82 was noticed upon rotation, requiring almost full right aileron to correct. the right main tank fuel gauge and totalizer had been deferred and the fuel tanks dripped for quantity. captain noted the md-82 had a maximum allowed lateral fuel imbalance of 1,500 lbs.

## Resolution
Reporter's concerns and frustrations are certainly warranted. Apparently the lack of adequate Maintenance trouble shooting prior to the fuel gauge MEL and a mis-read of the fuel drip quantity by a Contract Mechanic were major contributors to a very serious incident. But the reasons why contributors of this type occur have not been addressed.

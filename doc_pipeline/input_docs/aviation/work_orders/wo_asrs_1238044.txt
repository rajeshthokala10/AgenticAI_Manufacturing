Work Order: wo:asrs:1238044
Subsystem: both
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
r-22 instructor pilot experiences an engine failure shortly after takeoff and is able to auto rotate to an unused taxiway at the departure airport.

## Resolution
Was conducting a routine training flight and on departure experienced loss of engine power. Made a right turn crossing the extended center line of runway 15 at or below 100 feet AGL.  Executed a routine emergency landing on an unused adjacent taxiway.

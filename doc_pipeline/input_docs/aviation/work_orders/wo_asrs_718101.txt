Work Order: wo:asrs:718101
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
an a319 in cruise at fl360 had a cabin attendant pass a meal tray into the cockpit. soda can fell off tray shearing off #1 engine master switch in 'on' pos.

## Resolution
FLT ATTENDANT PASSED TRAYS CONTAINING SCHEDULED MEALS INTO COCKPIT. SODA CAN WAS ON ONE OF THE TRAYS AND FELL OFF THE TRAY LNDG ON THE #1 ENG MASTER SWITCH BREAKING IT OFF AT THE STEM. ALL ENGS CONTINUED TO OPERATE NORMALLY AND I NOTIFIED MAINT CTL WITH A QUERY FOR SHUTTING DOWN THE ENG. MR X TOLD US TO USE THE FIRE SWITCH IF NECESSARY ON ARR AT THE GATE TO SHUT DOWN #1 ENG. UPON ARR WE COULD NOT SECURE THE ENG SO SHUT IT DOWN USING THE FIRE SWITCH. MAINT DID NOT MEET US AT THE ACFT SO I CALLED THEM AND ASKED IF THERE WAS ANYTHING IN PARTICULAR THEY WANTED US TO DO AND WAS TOLD 'NO, JUST LEAVE THE BROKEN OFF PIECE ON THE CTR CONSOLE.'

Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
long ez pilot experiences loss of engine power during descent in humid conditions. carburetor heat does not solve the problem and the reporter elects to divert to a suitable airport. the engine continues to run and full power is restored once on the ground. carburetor ice is strongly suspected.

## Resolution
Carb ice is strongly suspected by this analyst also. The reporter has over 1,000 hours in this aircraft type and should have experienced this phenomenon previously.

Work Order: wo:asrs:616841
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a lancair single plt experienced an inflt engine failure and made a forced landing on a county road in zme's airspace.

## Resolution
CLBING THROUGH 9000 FT, THE ENG BEGAN RUNNING ROUGH. I PERFORMED A MAGNETO CHK. THE L MAGNETO WAS OK, BUT THE R MAGNETO SHUT OFF THE ENG. I SWITCHED BACK TO THE L MAGNETO, BUT THE ENG STILL WAS RUNNING ROUGH AND GETTING WORSE QUICKLY. I HAD FULL PWR APPLIED, BUT COULD NOT HOLD ALT. AT ABOUT 8000 FT, THE ENG STOPPED RUNNING. ATTEMPTS TO RESTART DID NOT WORK. I WENT THROUGH EMER PROCS, FOUND A COUNTY ROAD AND LANDED.

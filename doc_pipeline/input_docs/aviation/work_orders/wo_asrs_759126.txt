Work Order: wo:asrs:759126
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
c172 pax reports rough running engine 6 nm ese of zzz1 at 1000 feet. aircraft is able to climb to 2000 feet and return to departure arpt.

## Resolution
WE CONDUCTED A RUN-UP WITH NO NEGATIVE FINDINGS. WE TOOK OFF. WE TRANSITIONED E THRU THE CLASS 'C' AIRSPACE OF ZZZ1 AND PROCEEDED SE. APPROX 10 NM OUT WE ACTIVATED OUR FLT PLAN. IT WAS XA05 HRS AND THE ENG BEGAN TO RUN ROUGH. WE WERE AT 1000 FT IN LEVEL CRUISE FLT. WE ATTEMPTED TO CORRECT THE PROB CHKING FUEL, MIXTURE AND THROTTLE. WE ALSO TRIED CARB HEAT. NOTHING CORRECTED THE PROB. WE TURNED BACK TOWARD ZZZ1 AND BEGAN TO CLB. WE CONTACTED ZZZ1 AND ADVISED OF OUR SITUATION AND REQUESTED CLRNC BACK TO ZZZ. THIS WAS GRANTED AND WE ATTAINED 2000 FT. WE WERE NOW IN GLIDING DISTANCE OF ZZZ. WE CONTACTED ZZZ AND ADVISED OF OUR SITUATION AND OUR HIGH PATTERN ALT. WE WERE GIVEN CLRNC TO LAND AND DID SO WITHOUT INCIDENT. THE PLANE WAS TAKEN TO A MECH AND FOUND TO HAVE HAD NUMEROUS IGNITION PROBS THAT HAVE SINCE BEEN CORRECTED.

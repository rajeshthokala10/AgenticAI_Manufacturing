Work Order: wo:asrs:546878
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a piper 32r301 in cruise at 3500 declared an emer and diverted due to loss of power. both magneto units failed.

## Resolution
ENG BECAME ROUGH IN FLT--CHECKED ENG ROUGHNESS EMER CHKLIST THEN EXPERIENCED TOTAL LOSS OF POWER WENT THROUGH ENG LOSS OF POWER CHKLIST, ESTABLISHED GLIDE AND PROCEEDED TOWARDS NEAREST ARPT. LNDG AT ZZZ WITHOUT POWER. ACFT TOWED TO FBO MAINT WHO FOUND A DOUBLE MAGNETO FAILURE. CALLBACK CONVERSATION WITH RPTR REVEALED THE FOLLOWING INFO: THE RPTR STATED THE MAGNETOS WERE MANUFACTURED BY SLICK AND ON INSPECTION THE AREA OF THE BREAKER POINTS WAS ALL BURNED UP. THE RPTR SAID IT IS ALMOST IMPOSSIBLE TO BELIEVE THAT TWO MAGNETOS, TWO SEPARATE UNITS COULD FAIL AT THE SAME TIME. THE RPTR STATED THE AIRPLANE HAS ONLY 140 HRS TOTAL TIME.

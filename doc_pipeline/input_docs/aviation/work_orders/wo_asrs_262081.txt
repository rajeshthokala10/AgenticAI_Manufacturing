Work Order: wo:asrs:262081
Subsystem: both
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
an atx frtr plt left his aircraft unattended with one engine running.

## Resolution
DURING MY RUN-UP, I NOTICED MY L ENG MANIFOLD PRESSURE WOULD RISE AND L RPM GAUGE SHOWED LOWER RPM THAN NORMAL. I HAD JUST DEFROSTED 6 IN OF SNOW AND ICE FROM AIRPLANE IN HANGAR. I TAXIED TO MAINT AND PARKED IN FRONT OF THEIR MAINT HANGAR. THE LAST FEW DAYS MY BATTERY WOULD NOT KEEP A CHARGE. SO I LEFT MY R ENG RUNNING AND SHUT THE L ENG DOWN. APPLIED THE PARKING BRAKE AND EXITED THE ACFT WITH THE R ENG STILL AT 1000 RPM. I APPROACHED A MAINT PERSON TO ASSIST IN INSPECTING THE L ENG AND IT CHKED OK! THE REASON FOR THS RPT IS, I NOW REALIZE HOW DANGEROUS AND IRRESPONSIBLE IT WAS TO LEAVE MY AIRPLANE WITH ONE ENG RUNNING. IT WILL NEVER HAPPEN AGAIN IN MY AIRPLANE.

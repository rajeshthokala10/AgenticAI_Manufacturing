Work Order: wo:asrs:431188
Subsystem: both
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a commercial fixed wing small aircraft in cruise at 5500 ft declared an emer and diverted due to loss of engine pwr cause unknown.

## Resolution
I WAS FLYING XCOUNTRY FROM SAT TO RKP AT 5500 FT. APPROX 35 MI SE OF SAN ANTONIO, ENG LOST 1400 RPM AND THEN SURGED BACK TO CRUISE PWR SETTING OF 2400 RPM. THE DROP AND SURGE CONTINUED FOR THE NEXT 10 MINS. I CONTACTED ZHU AND ADVISED THEM OF MY INTENTIONS TO MAKE A PRECAUTIONARY LNDG AT CONNALLY RANCH, A PVT AIRSTRIP 25 MI S OF SAN ANTONIO. ZHU ASKED ME IF I WANTED TO DECLARE AN EMER, I SAID YES. I LANDED SAFETY AT CONNALLY WITHOUT INCIDENT. ON INSPECTION BY AN A&P ENG TESTED NORMAL. THE ANOMALY COULD NOT BE DUPLICATED.

Work Order: wo:asrs:1201384
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
b767 flight crew reports being dispatched in an aircraft with a history of fuel pump problems and the fwd equip cooling deferred and set to standby. as soon as the aircraft lifts off, eicas messages for fwd equip cooling and r center fuel pump low pressure are displayed. the pump is turned off and the fwd equip cooling is set to override but the crew elects to return to the departure airport.

## Resolution
The R Center fuel pump was not essential with the left pump functional and the aircraft could have kept flying as long as the Equipment Overheat warning did not reappear, but this aircraft was not suitable for a transatlantic crossing.

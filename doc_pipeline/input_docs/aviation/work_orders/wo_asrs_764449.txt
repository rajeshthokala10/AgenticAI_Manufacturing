Work Order: wo:asrs:764449
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a b737-300 inspector reports of verifying a #2 engine igniter lead replacement, but was unaware of the undocumented prsov valve sense line being disconnected by mechanic for access to the igniter lead.

## Resolution
WAS ASSIGNED #2 ENG INSPECTION THAT NIGHT. DURING INSPECTION FOUND LIGHT IGNITER LEAD CONDUCTOR OF EVIDENCE OF ARCING. MECH THEN REPLACED LEAD. THEN I INSPECTED ROUTING OF CABLE. MAINT MANUAL DOES NOT CALL FOR CTL AIR LINE DISCONNECTION FOR CABLE ROUTING. SO I WAS UNAWARE LINE WAS DISCONNECTED. MY FOCUS OF INSPECTION WAS ONLY THE CABLE ROUTING THROUGH ENG TO EXCITER BOX.

Work Order: wo:asrs:717103
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
b737 crew aborts tkof at den for fuel filter bypass with master caution.

## Resolution
WE WERE CONDUCTING A MAX GROSS WT TKOF FROM RWY 17L IN DEN. DURING THE TKOF ROLL WE ABORTED THE TKOF DUE TO A #2 ENG FUEL FILTER BYPASS LIGHT WITH MASTER CAUTION. TKOF WT WAS 129.7 LBS, SPDS WERE V1 163 KTS, VR 166 KTS, V2 170 KTS. ABORT WAS INITIATED AT APPROX 130 KTS. WE CONSULTED THE BRAKE COOLING CHART AND DETERMINED THEY WERE IN THE FUSE PLUG MELT ZONE. MAINT WAS CONTACTED AND EXPRESSED THEIR WISHES THAT WE TAXI TO THE GATE. BRAKES WERE NEVER SET AND ALL 4 TIRES DEFLATED EITHER APCHING THE GATE OR AT GATE. DISPATCH AND THE FLT OFFICE WERE NOTIFIED. WE WERE DEBRIEFED AND REMOVED FROM THE TRIP.

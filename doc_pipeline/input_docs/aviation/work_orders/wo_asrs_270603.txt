BARELY MADE IT BACK TO ARPT ON 1 ENG. RESTARTS (5) WERE UN-'SUCK'-CESSFUL. PLUGS WERE LATER FOUND FOULED. PLEASE DON'T EVER APPROVE A TWIN ENG ACFT WITH ONLY 1 HYD PUMP!

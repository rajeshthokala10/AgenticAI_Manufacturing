Work Order: wo:asrs:1028473
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a be-76 engine lost power after takeoff so the instructor took control, shut the engine down and returned to the airport. a mechanic had failed to secure the magneto after preflight maintenance.

## Resolution
This pilot did well and they were indeed fortunate that the aircraft was a twin engine machine. Hopefully, the outcome of this was a serious discussion with the Mechanic.

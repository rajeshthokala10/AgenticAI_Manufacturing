Work Order: wo:asrs:920333
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a b737-300 flight crew experienced problems with proper fuel distribution.

## Resolution
The MEL stated that no fuel was to be carried in the center tank. As the fueling was complete, the Fueler came up top and told me that he suspected that he put some gas in the center tank by accident.

I contacted Dispatch and told them that per the MEL that the fuel would have to be drained from the center tank. By the time this was complete, we departed approximately three hours late from the original departure time. We landed and I told the Agent of our problems with the center tank fuel. I went to the Ramp to talk with the Fueler about the problem and he assured me that no fuel was put into the center tank. The same process was applied for the next flight.

Work Order: wo:asrs:1006597
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a maintenace manager observed contract fuelers not in compliance with company procedures during the fueling operations of a company a320.

## Resolution
Apparently the Contracted Aircraft Fueling provider needs to implement an emergency training or recurrent training for all their Fuelers, to avoid a potentially major liability involving aircraft and people. Especially if a fuel fire should erupt as a result of an electrical static discharge during fueling caused by premature disconnection of the fuel ground bonding cable.

Work Order: wo:asrs:519240
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
b737-300 crew was unable to start #2 engine

## Resolution
ON ENG START OUT OF YVR, IGNITOR SWITCH SELECTED R, R ENG FAILED TO START. CONFERRED WITH MAINT CTLR. R IGNITOR NOT DEFERRABLE. ADVISED RETURN TO GATE FOR MAINT. CONTRACT MAINT WAS PERFORMED AND SIGNED OFF. START ON GATE WAS SUCCESSFUL. AFTER SUBSEQUENT PUSHBACK, R ENG AGAIN FAILED TO LIGHT OFF. AGAIN RETURNED TO GATE. CONTRACT MAINT AND MAINT CTLR RECOMMENDED ADDITIONAL R ENG START. START AT GATE WITH R IGNITOR STILL SELECTED WAS SUCCESSFUL. CONFERRED WITH MAINT CTLR AND CONTRACT MAINT AND THEY CONCLUDED ACFT WAS MECHANICALLY SOUND TO PROCEED. AFTER LNDG IN DEN, CONFERRED WITH LINE MAINT AND HE RECOMMENDED SECOND WRITE-UP TO ENSURE ACFT WAS CHKED ONCE MORE.

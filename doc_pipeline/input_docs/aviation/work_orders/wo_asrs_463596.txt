Work Order: wo:asrs:463596
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
an md80 had #2 engine fuel ctl replaced but fuel ctl input connecting rods were removed and the rig was disturbed.

## Resolution
ON NOV/XA/99, I INSTALLED A FUEL CTL ON ENG #XXXX, #2 POS. I REMOVED THE CONNECTING ROD FROM THE FUEL CTL END OF CONNECTING RODS ONLY TO FACILITATE REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION OF THE FUEL CTL. I DIDN'T REMOVE THE AFT END AT THE XOVER SHAFT AT GEAR BOX. REMOVAL OF AFT END OF CONNECTING ROD IS NOT REQUIRED FOR FUEL CTL CHANGE. ALL COTTER KEYS AND SAFETIES WERE REINSTALLED AS SEEN ON THE VIDEO THAT IS TAKEN AFTER THE ENG HAS GONE THROUGH AFTER TEST AND CHKED BY QUALITY CTL.

Work Order: wo:asrs:1114870
Subsystem: both
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
pa28 instructor pilot experiences a mysterious rpm drop while waiting for takeoff and elects to perform a second run-up. the run-up is normal and the flight takes off but rpm begins to decrease almost immediately. the tower is informed and the reporter is cleared for a short approach to the departure runway.

## Resolution
It is easy to see that the takeoff should have been rejected after the fact but was a difficult call during the takeoff. Better safe than sorry though.

Work Order: wo:asrs:594090
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
md80 experienced flames from engine during taxi-out at stl.

## Resolution
DURING SINGLE ENG TAXI, I ASKED FO TO START SECOND ENG. HE DID EVERYTHING NORMALLY EXCEPT FORGOT TO TURN ON IGNITION. UPON REALIZING THIS, HE PROPERLY PULLED START LEVER TO OFF AND CONTINUED TO MOTOR ENG TO CLR FUEL. NO LIMITS WERE EXCEEDED. AFTER MOTORING, A SECOND START WAS ACCOMPLISHED. AFTER COMPLETION, ANOTHER ACFT RPTED FLAMES IN THE TAILPIPE TO GND CTL. WE THEN SHUT DOWN THAT ENG. THAT SAME ACFT THEN RPTED WHITE SMOKE, BUT NO FIRE. GND CTL THEN CALLED FIRE TRUCKS AND MAINT WITHOUT PERMISSION OR PROMPTING BY US. GND TOLD US TO HOLD OUR POS. FIRE TRUCKS AND MAINT CAME OUT TO PLANE AND SPRAYED WATER INTO TAILPIPE. WE REPOSITIONED TO THE PAD AND HAD MAINT CHK OUT ENG. AFTER RUNNING ENG AND WRITING UP INCIDENT IN LOGBOOK, MECHS SAID ENG WAS FINE. MAINT ENGINEERING, THOUGH, ASKED US TO RETURN TO GATE, WHICH WE DID.

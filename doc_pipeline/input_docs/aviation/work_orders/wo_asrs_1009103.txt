Work Order: wo:asrs:1009103
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
b717 captain reports checking fuel just prior to descent for landing and finding all normal. during descent a fuel imbalance is annunciated with the left tank indicating 3,000 lbs less than the right. thinking fuel leak an emergency is declared with a diversion to a closer airport. during approach the fuel imbalance disappears and the left tank again indicates the same as the right.

## Resolution
This time differential and fuel loss would have indicated a very large fuel leak. Since the descent for landing had already commenced continuing to destination might have been the better choice. Had the leak been real the remaining fuel would have been gone before either airport could have been reached.

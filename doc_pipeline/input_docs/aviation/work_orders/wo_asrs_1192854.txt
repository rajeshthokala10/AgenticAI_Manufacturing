Work Order: wo:asrs:1192854
Subsystem: fuel_metering
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
c172 pilot experiences engine failure on short final after a low power descent from 3,500 feet. the aircraft lands short but rolls onto the runway without hitting any obstacles. the engine restarts on the second try and the aircraft is taxied clear of the runway.

## Resolution
If this aircraft had a carbureted engine, it is possible it became loaded up during the long descent. Running the engine up every minute or so during the descent is a recommended procedure to clear it and make sure it is still running.

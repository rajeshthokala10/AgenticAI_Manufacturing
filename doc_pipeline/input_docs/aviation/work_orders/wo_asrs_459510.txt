Work Order: wo:asrs:459510
Subsystem: ignition
Source: ASRS (Aviation Safety Reporting System)

## Complaint
a b747-400 was dispatched in non compliance with a known mechanical failure not entered in the aircraft flt log.

## Resolution
FAILED TO PLACE IN LOGBOOK A MAINT ITEM THAT STOPPED WORKING. FAR REQUIRES THIS BE PLACED IN LOGBOOK AT TIME OF FAILURES AND DISPATCH NOTIFIED. THIS WAS AN ALTERNATE START VALVE/IGNITION WHICH WE WROTE UP GOING INTO NRT. 'BY THE BOOK' WE SHOULD HAVE ENTERED INTO LOG -- I WAS UNAWARE OF THIS UNTIL IT WAS BROUGHT UP IN DISCUSSION WITH ANOTHER CREW. WILL GO BY FARS BETTER IN FUTURE.

The pricing, rebate tiers, and contract terms below are the *negotiated*
positions with strategic suppliers. They underwrite the FY26 margin plan
and must not be disclosed to:
  - Operators or shift supervisors (who only need part numbers + lead times).
  - Buyers issuing routine POs (who see catalogue prices, not contract tiers).
  - External parties or auditors without legal review.

1. SKF NORTH AMERICA — BEARINGS & SPINDLE PARTS

   Master Service Agreement: signed Sep 2024, expires Aug 2027.
   Annual minimum commit ............... $3.0M
   Volume rebate tier structure:
     $3.0M – $4.0M  →  4.0% rebate, paid quarterly
     $4.0M – $5.0M  →  6.5% rebate, paid quarterly
     $5.0M+         →  9.0% rebate + 30-day extended payment terms
   Most-favoured-customer clause: yes (signed addendum 2024-11).
   Tariff pass-through: capped at 50% above 8% duty.
   Single-source SKUs: BRG-7203, BRG-7305-P4, SPN-CNC-12-SET.

2. SIEMENS INDUSTRY — SERVO DRIVES & PLCS

   Frame Agreement: signed Jan 2025, expires Dec 2027.
   List price discount baseline ........ 22% off published list.
   Annual rebate (over $2.0M) .......... 3.5% calendar-year payout.
   Spare-part service level ............ 95% next-day for tier-A SKUs.
   Termination: 12 months written notice; liquidated damages cap $400k.
   Strategic note: qualification of Yaskawa as second-source is the only
   negotiating leverage we hold; do NOT signal weakness in renewal talks.

3. SANDVIK COROMANT — CUTTING TOOLS

   Consigned-stock agreement: revenue-recognised on consumption.
   Discount off list .................. 28% baseline, 33% above $1.5M.
   Tool-life guarantee penalty ........ $0.05/insert below stated
                                         tool-life (rarely invoked).
   New-product trial window ........... 90 days, no obligation to buy.
   Confidentiality clause: pricing not to be shared with any party
   competing with Sandvik (i.e. Kennametal, Iscar reps).

4. PARKER HANNIFIN — HYDRAULICS & PNEUMATICS

   Renewal in negotiation — Procurement Manager to lead, target close
   June 2026. Internal walk-away: 25% off list at $1.2M+ commit.
   Backup: Bosch Rexroth (samples qualified, lead-time disadvantage
   of 3-4 weeks on tier-A SKUs — do NOT disclose this gap to Parker).
